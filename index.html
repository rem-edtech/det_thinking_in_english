bolt Why "Thinking in English" is Paramount

Most ESL students read an English prompt, translate it to their native language, plan their answer, and then translate it back.

On the DET, this process is too slow. You lose up to 30% of your time just translating in your head. Training your brain to link thoughts directly to English words is the most effective way to protect your production score.
